The Insurance sector is a vibrant and critical part of the UK economy, adding circa £30bn each year. It is the largest Insurance market in Europe, the fourth largest in the world and employs over 300,000 people.

To the outsider, the UK Insurance marketplace can appear to be complex and confusing. Knowing your way around the industry and who to speak to, can be a minefield. If you are looking to find your way around or to be introduced into the key players, then Critical Friend Partnership may be able to help.
